Synchro LOS Parser

Drop in Synchro reports (.txt) and get an Excel LOS summary with scenario/period tabs.

Click or drag & drop your Synchro reports here

Accepts multiple .txt files (e.g., 22700.00 EX AM.txt, 22700.00 NB PM.txt)

Preview (first 20 rows)

How to use

  1. Export your Synchro reports as .txt files (File → Reports → Text Report).
  2. Name files with scenario and period (e.g., 22700.00 EX AM.txt, 22700.00 BD MIT SAT.txt).
  3. Drop all files above or click to browse (you can select multiple files).
  4. Click Generate Excel File.
  5. The Excel workbook downloads with tabs named from the filenames (e.g., "EX AM").
Supports TWSC (unsignalized) and Signalized intersections · Synchro 6–12
